Sharing as a page admin should be as easy as sharing as a member

It wasn't easy to post as a Page on Linkedin, until recently. Catering to a growing number of Company posts and engagement, Sharing and Pages team united to bring Page post functionality to the main share box on on the consumer side of Linkedin.

02

Optimizing for member and pages, as well as visibility forced us to re-think our share box.

We explored several directions given time constraints and other dependencies. We found that a second call to action using existing components was the best path forward. It Easiest to build, allows for discovery, and optimizes for member only (still ~88% of overall original posts) — with account & privacy toggling living independently. Ultimately, this leveraged three scalable lock up cases that align to existing products and shared Art Deco system components.
