Helping merchants across Google

UX, interaction and visual design. Worked with several other designers on this project. We defined the UX strategy alongside PM vision thinking. At first a small team with low visibility, growing to a vast Google-wide effort with commercials around the world.

I defined features, interaction patterns, worked with motion design and a handful of iOS, Android and front-end engineers to create the very first version of this product.

Challenge

Merchants don't have a clear path to manage or promote their business on Google

In 2014, 56% of local retailers hadn’t claimed their Google listing. Yelp and Yahoo are close behind and moving fast. So, what opportunities are in this space?

Right now, any attempt is hidden from the user and it’s not obvious where all of this info surfaces once provided. There’s no 1:1 correlation between editing and what’s being shown on Google.

Research

Small businesses wanted to, but had too little time or understanding on how to update their hours, photos or address on Maps

We took a look at some existing patterns as well as a few competitive products like Yelp and Trip Advisor.

Hours, service location, address - those types of needs we saw the highest number for. Most update these across other products but weren't aware of how to do it with Google.

Other local products such as Yelp & Foursquare (shown) have simpler editors within their consumer apps, but don’t cater to navigation & transit like Google does (less complex, lighter APK’s etc).

Google still had technical challenges to overcome for verifying businesses which were difficult to manage outside of the U.S. This launched did not aim to correct every part of verification logistics including Post Card verification.

We needed a simple way to collate these needs into a single location

Explorations

I also worked with illustrators, designers + researchers to identify + iterate across emerging market needs, including localization

Brazil, India, Russia -- and so on are areas of the world where the data is sparse and folks need extra help online.

To build a product scalable between web + mobile systems. Most use inline editor patterns, and could share various UI directly from Google Maps and Search already.

Ultimately and after multiple rounds of research and a handful of real prototypes - we decided to target small businesses and let our desktop sister product handle chains and bulk actions. The mobile approach was aimed at taking advantage of device-specific features (eg mobility and camera), encouraging merchants to update their information and share content no matter their location.

Visual + Interaction Design

We had to merge Google+ with Search + Maps in order for this to work, using their framework + design system to support these needs

Supporting both web + mobile platforms

Outcome

Ultimately supported in over 100 languages + home to 1B verified places

A stand alone app that could be accessed on any device, anywhere in the world, push notifications and inform users of all the right info at the right time.

Now located in all app store marketplaces, one can easily access, manage and take control of their listing as others see it across all Google surfaces.

We had successfully traversed away from the social side of Google, and now it was time to set sail on a new experience that wasn't beholden to another platform. From bookings to enhanced photo control, we'll see you in the future of online management and promotion.

Making it super easy to get in + learn about key features

New entry points across Maps, a marketplace position in every app store, and a guided walkthrough on what you should know about this product.

In-app feature discovery using vetted Google patterns, and a marketing story for any business owner to grasp. Your photos, info and accessibility are important to your business -- and important to Google and quality data. That ultimately helps us, everywhere.

As well as learn about and maintain your listing - for free

Get in and get out, or stick around and track some metrics. This app allows users to take full advantage of their online Google listing right from their phone or desktop computer.

Moderate reviews, photos and other info essential to keeping your listing noticed and up to date. Hire a professional photographer, set up 360 Tours, or even hook up your Adwords account and lump everything together.

Unlock the full potential of Google with Google My Business.
