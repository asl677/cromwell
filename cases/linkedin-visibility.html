making it easier to share as the right person to the right audience on linkedin

linkedin members often found themselves not sure what or where they are sharing to. What does 'Anyone' mean and what else can I do here? This led to drop off and frustration, and more times than not, sharing content to the wrong audience.

03

I worked with the sharing team, a UX writer and researcher, and several engineers to iterate and validate several changes to the core sharing experience.

We found that members wanted to better understand the visibility settings, as a constant source of confusion as to who they were sharing content to. The clearer, the better, the more confident the post.

Companies and pages had a large role in the ecosystem but not a large role in how they share that content. From jobs to promotions, companies - small and large - had to hunt and peck to share as themselves, so we changed that.
