Local Bookings on Google

02

we needed a flexible system to handle our local verticals and potentially 350m places on google. leveraging available and competitive patterns, we then prioritized work based on query volume, and how we planned to work with others. we then developed a template flow that could scale between several verticals, to include dining, fitness, beauty/wellness, hotels, events and services.

00

We tested several flows, patterns and multiple rounds of research from surveys to cafe and rapid user studies, using interactive prototypes.

00

i worked closely with another designer to create a scalable partner framework, ui kit and lay a foundation for future transactional support. finding that icon-driven actions didn't scale well between locales, and didn't allow for customized strings for specific verticals, moving us to a text-only approach for entry points within the place sheet. taking cues from competitors like resy and open table, we then partnered with products like mindbody to support and end to end booking experience all within google.

our v1 was a mobile-optimized mvp - supporting web and mobile web, now on ios and android. the ux team worked closely with visual design leads and production designers to standardize components into stickersheets, creating a shareable system for other partners and teams to leverage and test against.
