viewing videos on google maps & search just got a little easier

There were a lot of videos being uploaded to maps. Not enough between verticals to provided a dedicated place but enough to scale a solution to handle the cases.

03

I worked closely with the UGC (user generated content) team in Japan, and another designer on this project.

We found that there was a growing number of videos overall, but not by vertical such as commodities and chains. Yet, a surging amount of content from restaurants, venues and other navigational searches.

This UI allowed for us to scale into a dedicated tab, flag a specific video and scrub through the content, taking cues from other products like Google Photos.
