Hi there — I'm Alex, a Product Designer living in San Francisco.

Over a decade of experience, having worked with Google, Ueno, Sapient, Journeys & Zippo.

Web, mobile web, iOS, Android, branding, illustration, creative direction, rapid prototyping, front-end development, mentorship -- just a few of the things I've been helping others with over the years. I've got a strong passion for bold visual design and smooth, delightful interactions that uses simple motion design to create unique user experiences.

From wireframes and pitch decks to UI Kits and extensive prototyping -- I've been blessed to work with some awesome players on some awesome projects over the years. From agencies to start ups, features to frameworks, Sapient to Google - it's been a crazy ride, and the opportunities continue to grow. I also enjoy mentorship and pushing the boundaries, all while typically using data and research to back up my crazy ideas. If in the Bay Area, don't hesitate to reach out for coffee.
