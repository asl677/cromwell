Expertise
I like solving problems at scale, that last for years to come. It's quite rewarding to create solutions to everyday problems, ultimately adding new layers of simplicity to our daily lives. I've tackled quintessential needs such as day planning, dining, B2B engagement, and improving on Local Search across Google Maps.
